Forza Horizon 4 is an open world racing video game developed by Playground Studio and published by Micrsoft Studios. It was released on 2 October 2018 on Xbox one  and Microsoft windows after being announced at Xobox E3 2018 conference The game is set in a fictionalised representation of the United Kingdom. It is the fourth Forza Horizon title and eleventh instalment in the Forza seris. The game is noted for its introduction of changing seasons to the series.
